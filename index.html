Research Areas

Our Core Research

We lead the future of autonomous industry by integrating Digital Twins, Generative AI, and Intelligent Robotics to create seamless Cyber-Physical Systems.

🧠

Generative AI

Advancing human-robot collaboration by integrating Large Language Models (LLMs) and Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models to create next-generation digital assistants capable of understanding complex industrial instructions.

🦾

Robotics

Developing high-efficiency AI sensing and real-time point cloud analysis to enhance robotic gripping capabilities, implementing "Perception at the Edge" for autonomous decision-making in dynamic environments.

🌐

International Standard

Bridging the gap between virtual and physical environments using NVIDIA Isaac Sim and synthetic data, while implementing Asset Administration Shell (AAS) to build reconfigurable smart manufacturing systems.

👥

Human Supporting System

Designing intelligent environments where humans and robots coexist safely, utilizing reinforcement learning-based adaptive control and wearable monitoring to define dynamic safety zones and worker support systems.
